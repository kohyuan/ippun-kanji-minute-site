Before you write

If you're reporting a problem, including the following will help us respond quickly:

  • Your device model (for example, iPhone 14 Pro)
  • Your iOS version
  • The app version (found in Settings → About inside the app)
  • A short description of what happened and what you expected

Frequently asked

Do I need an account to play?

No. ippun works entirely without an account. Your progress is saved on your device.

What if I delete the app?

Because progress is saved only on your device, reinstalling the app will start you fresh. Cloud sync is planned for a future update.

How do I turn off analytics?

Open Settings inside the app and switch off Help improve ippun. When disabled, no further data is collected.

I found a mistake in a translation or reading.

Please email us the compound and the correction. Every entry is reviewed by native speakers, but mistakes do slip through — we're grateful for the catch.

Will ippun come to Android or iPad?

Android is planned shortly after the iPhone launch. An iPad-native layout will follow. We'll post updates on this page.

Is ippun free?

Yes, with no ads and no forced sign-in. Optional paid features may arrive in a future update, but the core experience will remain free.

Response time

We aim to reply within two business days. Thank you for your patience.
