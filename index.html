About

A small, focused practice.

ippun is built around a simple idea: a single minute of careful attention, repeated, builds real fluency. Each round gives you an anchor character and a tray of drifting kanji — your job is to find the compounds that fit, before the timer runs down.

No streaks to guilt you. No notifications to interrupt you. Just a calm, repeatable loop you can return to whenever you have the time.

What's inside
